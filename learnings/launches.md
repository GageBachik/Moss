# Launch Learnings

## App Store Optimization
- App name: Include primary keyword, keep it memorable
- First paragraph of description is most important for search
- Screenshots should tell a story (problem → solution → delight)
- Use all 100 characters in the keywords field

## Submission Notes
- Health/fitness apps get extra scrutiny -- ensure no medical claims
- Always have privacy policy and terms live BEFORE submitting
- First submission takes 24-48 hours for review typically
- Rejection reasons should be logged for future reference

## Landing Page
- Keep it simple: one page, one CTA
- Match the app's visual theme
- Include app screenshots or mockups
- Privacy policy and terms linked from footer
