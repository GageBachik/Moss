# Scout Learnings

## Trend Sources (ranked by reliability)
1. TikTok Creator Search Insights -- best for discovering real user pain points
2. App Store top charts -- best for identifying category gaps
3. Google Trends -- best for confirming trend momentum (not just spikes)

## Patterns That Work
- Apps solving a specific daily frustration (not broad lifestyle changes)
- Utility apps with clear, single-purpose value propositions
- Apps in growing categories with low competition (check ASO difficulty)
- Niches with 0-7 competitors where no app has >500 ratings -- best validation signal
- Apps riding TikTok wellness/lifestyle trends with 100M+ views but no dedicated app

## Patterns That Fail
- Apps requiring large user bases to be useful (social/network effects)
- Apps in heavily regulated categories (health claims, financial advice) without clear compliance path
- Apps competing directly with Apple's built-in apps
- Copycat apps with no differentiation from top 3 competitors
- Concepts where the niche already has 8+ iOS apps (even if they're mediocre) -- validator will kill these
- Concepts targeting spending-averse audiences (budget trackers, pan tracking) -- monetization math fails

## ASO Notes
- Primary keyword should be in the app name
- Difficulty > 50 is almost always a losing battle for a new app
- Popularity < 20 means not enough people are searching
